# Claude at TDM — New Starter Onboarding

Welcome to The Digital Maze. This guide gets you up and running with Claude in under 30 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Install Claude Desktop (5 min)

1. Download from **claude.com/download**
2. Sign in with your TDM Google account (`@thedigitalmaze.com`)
3. You'll be added to the TDM workspace automatically

---

## Step 2: Know Which Claude Tool To Use (2 min)

There are three versions of Claude. Use the right one for the job.

| If You're Doing... | Use | How To Access |
|---|---|---|
| Writing an email, asking a question, brainstorming | **Claude Chat** | claude.ai in browser |
| Building/editing files, working on a project | **Claude Code** | Inside Claude Desktop, "Code" tab |
| Organising files, creating PowerPoints, running reports | **Claude Cowork** | Inside Claude Desktop, "Cowork" tab |

**Rule of thumb:** Start with Chat. Move to Code or Cowork only when Chat isn't enough.

---

## Step 3: Connect Your Tools (10 min)

Open **Settings > Customize > Connectors** and connect the ones relevant to your role:

### Everyone At TDM Should Connect
- **Gmail** (your TDM email)
- **Google Calendar** (your TDM calendar)
- **Google Drive** (TDM shared drives)
- **Slack** (TDM workspace)
- **Monday.com** (project boards)

### Connect If Your Role Needs Them

| Role | Connect |
|---|---|
| **SEO Team** | Semrush, Ahrefs, Google Search Console, Google Analytics |
| **PPC Team** | Google Ads, Meta Ads, Google Analytics |
| **Web Dev** | GitHub, Vercel, Sentry |
| **Web Design** | Figma, Canva |
| **Client Services** | Otter.ai (meeting notes) |
| **Finance** | Xero, Stripe |

For custom connectors not in the default list, ask the admin (Liam or Scott) for the URL.

---

## Step 4: Set Up Your Personal Preferences (5 min)

Go to **Settings > Profile**. Paste this starter template and customise:

```
I work at The Digital Maze, a 20-25 person UK digital agency. 
My role is [ROLE].

When writing for clients or teammates, use British English. 
Be concise and practical. No fluff.

Explain in plain English unless I ask for technical depth.
Challenge me if I'm being vague or over-complicating things.
```

Adjust the role and any personal preferences. You can update this any time.

---

## Step 5: Create Your First Project (5 min)

Projects let Claude remember context across conversations.

1. In Claude Desktop, click **New Project**
2. Name it (e.g., "My Daily Ops" or "Client: [Name]")
3. In the project's **Instructions**, add:
   - What the project is for
   - Any reference documents (upload brand guides, style guides)
   - Tone/style preferences
4. Every conversation in that project uses these automatically

---

## Step 6: Understand the Boundaries (2 min)

### What Claude Can Do
- Read files you give it access to
- Write emails, reports, copy in your voice
- Analyse data, summarise meetings
- Build and edit websites/apps (with Code)
- Work with client tools via Connectors

### What Claude Cannot Do
- Access your accounts without you signing in
- Make purchases or financial transactions
- Share client data outside the conversation
- Run without your approval on risky actions

### Company Rules
- **Never paste client passwords or API keys** into Claude
- **Double-check anything that goes to a client** before sending
- **Use TDM accounts for TDM work** (not personal Google, etc.)
- **Client data stays in client-specific projects** (no cross-contamination)

---

## Step 7: Your First Prompt

Try this as your first Claude Chat message to get a feel for it:

> "Hi Claude. I'm [name], [role] at The Digital Maze. Today my priorities are [1, 2, 3]. Help me prioritise and suggest a rough schedule for the day."

You'll see how Claude uses your profile, your project context, and connected tools all at once.
